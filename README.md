X-Inactivation Re-analysis: RAP-seq, RNA-seq, and GSEA

A re-analysis of publicly available RAP-seq and RNA-seq data from the van Bemmel et al. (2019, Nature Genetics) study on X-chromosome inactivation (XCI).
